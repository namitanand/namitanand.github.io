Namit Anand

\(\blacktriangleright\) I’m a Staff Scientist at NASA Ames’ Quantum AI Lab (QuAIL) and KBR. My research lies at the intersection of quantum information theory, many-body physics, and quantum error correction.

\(\blacktriangleright\) In Summer 2022, I received my PhD in Physics from the University of Southern California under the supervision of Paolo Zanardi. I was fortunate to be coadvised by Aaron Lauda and Todd Brun.
